Who We Are

FutureOZ Consultancy Sydney Representing World Premier Institutes in Australia.

FutureOZ is established in 2015 and focusing to provide remarkable services to our Clients. QEAC Certified K-401 Counsellor and we do understand the need of right advice.Being in the service industry our motto at FutureOz is “Students Comes First”. It is our constant endeavor to

provide accurate and reliable information to our clients and help them make an informed decision. We work with quality providers at safe and friendly destinations to ensure positive career outcomes for students.We are working with the mission to provide students with unlimited opportunities to accomplish

career goals through quality education.To accomplish this we offer first-hand assistance to both local & international students including airport pickup services, helping arrange home-stays and even opening bank accounts. We also stay in regular contact with our students and liaise with institute and parents in

monitoring students' progress.

FutureOz dedicate to the cause of providing personalized consultancy to students as well as parents, at all stages of studying abroad, from School to University.For students choosing to study in other parts of Australia or overseas, we ensure that we only work with education providers that can offer a high standard

of academic achievement and student support, and we would maintain regular contact with our students.Every student has individual requirements, but in order to ensure a well-informed choice there are almost always special factors to consider, including a student's academic background, age, knowledge of

English, when to come to the university or college, the length of the student's stay in the university/college and what, where and how he/she wishes to study. We assist students with all aspects of planning and arranging for their study overseas.

FutureOz prime impetus always will be to maintain professional standards in services.
